Introduction

RainTree Immigration ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us when you use our website or engage our visa and immigration consultancy services.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you provide, such as your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Travel and immigration documents you submit to us for visa or work permit processing, such as passport copies, photographs, and supporting financial documents.
  • Messages and details you submit through our contact form.
  • Basic website usage information, such as pages visited.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to respond to your enquiries, prepare and submit visa or immigration applications on your behalf, communicate with you about your case, and improve our services.

Sharing Your Information

We share your information only where necessary to provide our services — for example, with embassies, consulates, or government immigration authorities as part of your application. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information, including sensitive travel documents, from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information held by us by contacting us using the details below.

Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to support core functionality. We do not currently use cookies for advertising or third-party tracking.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
